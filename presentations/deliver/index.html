What is Delivery Analytics?

Delivery analytics optimizes the final stages of the supply chain to enhance customer experience:

  • Customer satisfaction - Understanding and meeting expectations
  • Order fulfillment - On-Time In-Full (OTIF) performance
  • Network design - Optimal facility locations
  • Transportation - Cost optimization and routing
  • Last mile - Delivery time prediction and optimization
