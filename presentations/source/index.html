What is Sourcing Analytics?

Sourcing analytics encompasses all data-driven processes related to procuring materials and services from suppliers:

  • Selecting and managing suppliers - Finding the right partners for your supply chain
  • Monitoring quality and performance - Ensuring suppliers meet standards
  • Managing compliance and documentation - Meeting regulatory requirements
  • Assessing and mitigating risks - Protecting against disruptions
  • Planning capacity and collaboration - Aligning supply with demand

SOURCE Use Cases

01 Supplier Performance

Metrics and KPIs for evaluating supplier effectiveness, including on-time delivery, quality ratings, and cost performance.

02 Supplier Risk

Analytics for identifying and mitigating supplier risks through risk scoring, scenario analysis, and network evaluation.

03 Capacity Planning

Aligning supplier capabilities with demand through lead time analysis, constraint optimization, and collaborative planning.

04 Compliance Tracking

Document management and regulatory compliance using OCR, NLP, and automated compliance verification systems.

05 Raw Materials Quality

Quality assurance for incoming materials through statistical quality control, root cause analysis, and predictive methods.
