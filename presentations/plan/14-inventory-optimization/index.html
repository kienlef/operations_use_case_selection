Inventory Optimization

Strategic stock management for cost efficiency

What Is It?

Inventory Level Optimization strategically manages stock to meet demand while minimizing costs.

  • Demand forecasting - Predict future requirements
  • Lead time assessment - Factor in supplier delivery times
  • Optimal quantities - Right amount at the right time
  • Cost balancing - Avoid overstocking and stockouts

Business Objectives

Cost Efficiency

Reduce storage, insurance, spoilage

Working Capital

Improve cash flow

Responsive SC

Adapt to demand changes
