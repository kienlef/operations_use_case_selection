What is Supply Chain Planning?

Supply chain planning analytics enables data-driven decisions across strategic, tactical, and operational levels:

  • Demand forecasting - Predicting future customer demand
  • Inventory optimization - Balancing stock levels and costs
  • Multi-echelon planning - Coordinating across supply chain tiers
  • S&OP alignment - Integrating sales and operations plans
  • Risk management - Building supply chain resilience

Planning Analytics Themes

Data Foundation

  • Historical sales & demand
  • Inventory levels & costs
  • Lead time data
  • Market & external factors
  • Capacity constraints

Methods

  • Time-series (ARIMA, Prophet)
  • Machine learning forecasting
  • Optimization algorithms
  • Monte Carlo simulation
  • Scenario analysis

Key Metrics

  • Forecast accuracy (MAPE)
  • Inventory turns
  • Service level / Fill rate
  • Days of inventory
  • Plan adherence
